How I Work

Consultation/Brainstorm

I get to know the project.

This can be as simple as a conversation between you and I. I find out what your goals for the project are. We bounce ideas off each other and you tell me what you like and don't like. I will also tell you know what works and doesn't like.

Problem Solving

I sit in my office, turn on some Chopin, and THINK.

This is where my experience and skills as a professional come into play. I take our ideas and thoughts and turn them into solutions. I sketch, prepare wireframes, and create dummy code.
